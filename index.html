charging stations in Kerala - India's best electric vehicles news portal

Kerala State Electricity Board (KSEB) is embarking on a transformative initiative to establish a network of 70 Electric Vehicle (EV) charging stations across the picturesque landscapes of Kerala, as reported by India's premier electric vehicles news portal. This forward-thinking move by KSEB not only underscores the state's commitment to sustainable and eco-friendly transportation but also positions Kerala at the forefront of the burgeoning electric mobility revolution. As the demand for electric vehicles continues to rise, these strategically positioned charging stations will play a pivotal role in supporting the widespread adoption of EVs, fostering environmental consciousness, and propelling Kerala towards a greener and more energy-efficient future. Stay tuned to the latest developments on this groundbreaking endeavor as KSEB leads the charge towards a sustainable and electrifying tomorrow.
